Komurcu Kula Anatolian Rug

Tittle

Komurcu Kula Anatolian rug

Description

A profusion of stylized tulips, carnations and other classical Turkish flowers. A very nice example of this increasingly difficult-to-find type, made of wool and natural dyes, double knot technique used

Category

Handicrafts
